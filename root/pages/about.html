About the school

The Yale School of Art community extends its creative influence far beyond campus. Through exhibitions, talks, and projects worldwide, our alumni, faculty, and students showcase their talent and innovation on a global stage. Whether in international galleries or public workshops, these events highlight the impact of Yale artists across the world. Explore "Yale School of Art in the World" to stay connected and experience the far-reaching influence of our vibrant community.

Our History

For decades, the Yale School of Art has fostered innovation, shaping generations of artists and creative leaders. It has long been a vibrant space for dialogue, exploration, and the pursuit of artistic excellence, inspiring students to push boundaries and refine their craft with purpose and vision.

Support the Arts

Your contributions help empower the next generation of artists and designers. With your generous support, the Yale School of Art can continue to provide scholarships, cutting-edge facilities, mentoring opportunities, and platforms for innovation and artistic growth.

Visit Us

Nestled within the historic Yale University campus, the Yale School of Art thrives in a community where art and academia intersect.
